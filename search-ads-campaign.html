What we did

We ran the full search campaign end to end: keyword research, campaign structure, ad copywriting, responsive search ads, bidding strategy, and audience and location targeting, so every ad was in front of people actively searching for the client's services.

We continuously monitored keyword performance, optimized bids, and refined ad copy to improve click-through rate while maintaining a cost-efficient CPC. The campaign focused on attracting high-intent users searching for the client's services — and delivered 4,580+ impressions, 144 clicks, a 3.14% CTR, and an average CPC of around ₹10.01.

Tools used

Google Ads, Google Keyword Planner, Google Analytics, and Google Tag Manager.

Skills demonstrated: PPC advertising, search engine marketing (SEM), keyword research, conversion-focused ad copy, campaign optimization, budget management, and performance analysis.
